### 🧠 About Me

Modern software is built on invisible systems — distributed services, resilient infrastructure, efficient algorithms, and performance-conscious engineering. Understanding and building those foundations is what drives my learning.

My work centers on **backend engineering**, building scalable services with **Java and Spring Boot**, while continuously strengthening my understanding of **system design, distributed computing, databases, networking, and cloud-native infrastructure**.

Beyond frameworks, I invest heavily in **computer science fundamentals** — algorithms, operating systems, computer architecture, concurrency, networking, database internals, and performance engineering — because they explain *why* large-scale systems behave the way they do.

**Competitive programming** sharpens my algorithmic reasoning, problem decomposition, and analytical thinking through **Codeforces** and **AtCoder**.

My long-term focus extends toward **distributed infrastructure, intelligent computing, performance-critical systems, and quantitative computing** — domains where scalability, correctness, latency, and reliability converge.
